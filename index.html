Welcome to the 2nd CITEDH-R1 Regional Research Conference in ITE & IT Skills Competitions (CRRCISC) 2024!

We are delighted to extend a heartfelt invitation to all esteemed participants, educators, professionals, and enthusiasts to join us for an enriching experience at the forefront of Information Technology innovation. Hosted by the COUNCIL OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION DEANS AND HEADS- REGION 1 (CITEDH-R1), this conference promises to serve as a dynamic platform where ideas converge, innovation thrives, and collaboration flourishes.

Featuring an engaging lineup of topics encompassing computer science, information technology, data mining, educational technology, and more, CRRCISC 2024 provides fertile ground for learning, networking, and collaboration. Whether you’re a seasoned expert, a budding professional, or a passionate advocate, this event offers invaluable opportunities to exchange insights, forge connections, and contribute to the advancement of IT education and research.

CRRCISC 2024 is proud to introduce prominent personalities in the field, dedicated to exploring the THEME: “Emerging Trends in Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency, Cyber Security, and Data-driven Decision Making for Research Innovation.”
